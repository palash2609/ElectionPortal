Frequently Asked Questions

How can I register to vote?
To register to vote, you need to fill out a voter registration form available at your nearest electoral office or online on the official website of the Election Commission of India. You will need to provide proof of identity and address.
When are the next Lok Sabha elections scheduled?
The schedule for Lok Sabha elections varies. You can check the Election Commission of India's website for the latest election schedule.
How can I apply for a voter ID card?
You can apply for a voter ID card through the online portal provided by the Election Commission of India. Alternatively, you can visit your nearest electoral office to apply in person.
What documents are required for voter registration?
To register as a voter, you need to provide proof of identity and proof of address. Documents such as Aadhar card, passport, driving license, or utility bills can be submitted as proof.
How do I check my voter registration status?
You can check your voter registration status online on the Election Commission of India's website by entering your details such as name, date of birth, and state.
Can I vote if I am living abroad?
Yes, eligible Indian citizens living abroad can vote in elections through the "Election Commission of India Overseas" program. They need to register as overseas voters and cast their vote through postal ballots.
What is the voting age in India?
The minimum voting age in India is 18 years. Any citizen who has attained the age of 18 on the qualifying date is eligible to register as a voter and cast their vote.
How can I find my polling station?
You can find your polling station by using the "Voter Helpline" app provided by the Election Commission of India. Alternatively, you can check the polling station details on the electoral roll available at your nearest electoral office.
What is NOTA?
NOTA stands for "None of the Above." It is an option provided on electronic voting machines (EVMs) for voters who do not wish to vote for any of the candidates contesting in the election.
Can I vote without a voter ID card?
Yes, you can vote without a voter ID card if your name is on the electoral roll and you have an alternative form of identification recognized by the Election Commission of India.
