Steps for the Voting Process

  1. Visit your designated polling station on the day of the election.
  2. Present your voter ID card and get your identity verified by the polling officials.
  3. Receive the ballot paper or use electronic voting machine (EVM) for casting your vote.
  4. Mark your vote securely and confidentially without disclosing it to anyone.
  5. Deposit the marked ballot paper or confirm your vote on the EVM and exit the polling booth.
  6. Thank you for participating in the democratic process!
